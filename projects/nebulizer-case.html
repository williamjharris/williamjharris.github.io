Nebulizer Carrying Case

A custom-designed carrying case for nebulizer treatment equipment, enhancing portability and accessibility for patients on the go.

2025
SOLIDWORKS CAD Fabrication Medical Device

Overview

Collaborated in a two-person team to design and fabricate a nebulizer carrying case that enhances treatment accessibility and mobility. The case was designed entirely in SOLIDWORKS and features several functional attachments to improve the user experience.

Design Features

  • Rotating ergonomic handle for comfortable carrying
  • Removable lid for easy access to the nebulizer
  • Integrated medicine capsule compartment
  • Ventilated case walls for airflow during operation

Assembly Drawing

CAD & Fabrication

The entire case was modeled in SOLIDWORKS with full parametric design, allowing rapid iteration on dimensions and attachment geometry. The final design was fabricated using a combination of 3D printing and laser cutting.

Impact

The case makes nebulizer treatment more practical for patients who need to travel or be mobile during treatment sessions. By integrating storage for medication and allowing the device to operate within the case, it removes several friction points from the treatment workflow.
