// about

I'm a sophomore studying Biomedical Engineering at the University of Colorado Boulder. I'm passionate about developing technologies that improve healthcare outcomes — from wearable biosensors to CAD-fabricated medical device accessories.

I have hands-on experience in SOLIDWORKS (Certified SOLIDWORKS Associate), Python, VBA, and embedded systems. My projects range from a real-time heart rate monitor built with an ESP32 and MAX30102 sensor to a nebulizer carrying case designed to enhance treatment accessibility. I also volunteer in the Post Anaesthesia Care Unit at St. Anthony North Hospital, supporting patient recovery alongside RNs.

I'm an Esteemed Scholar and two-time Dean's List honoree, and a member of the CU Biomedical Engineering Society. When I'm not in the lab or the library, I'm probably running trails or tinkering with a new sensor breakout board.
